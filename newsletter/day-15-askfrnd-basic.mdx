**An AI assistant that actually knows what you're looking at.**

See that panel on the right side of every page? That's **AskFrnd** — your AI assistant inside frndOS. It's not a chatbot bolted on the side: it's **context-aware**. Open it from Insights and it can analyze your dashboard data ("what were my top 3 posts last month?"). Open it from Studio and it's in creative mode. Same question, different place, more relevant answer.

What you can do with it:

- **Interrogate your data** — "why did the summer campaign outperform the winter one?"
- **Generate images and videos** right in the chat — pick a model, describe what you want, iterate with follow-ups
- **Brainstorm** — "10 caption ideas for a Ramadan campaign for a youth banking brand"

A quick word on **credits**: AI actions draw from your workspace's credit pool. Cost varies by model and action — image and video generation cost more than text — and it's shown after generation. Failed generations cost nothing.

One honest note: for guaranteed brand-perfect output, the KV Generator is still your tool — Brand Knowledge doesn't fully flow into chat generation yet.
