**One workspace, your whole team — clients included.**

frndOS gets dramatically more useful when your team is inside. There are two ways in:

**Invite directly.** From **Workspace Settings → Members**, send an invite to anyone's email and pre-assign their role. If they're new to frndOS, the sign-up form locks to that email — so the invitation can't drift to the wrong person. Perfect for proactively adding stakeholders and clients.

**Let them request to join.** Enable **workspace visibility** and team members can search for your workspace and request access. You (and other admins) get a notification, and nobody joins without your approval. Visibility is off by default — turning it on only makes the workspace findable, never your content.

Roles keep things tidy: **Owner** and **Admin** manage the workspace, **Editors** create the work, and **Viewer/Guest** is read-only — ideal for clients who need visibility, not edit rights (and zero risk of accidental changes).
