Hi [first_name],

Before you can add your brand, invite teammates, or run your first creative — you need a workspace.

Think of it as your team's home in frndOS. Everything your team creates, analyzes, and collaborates on lives inside it.

Setting it up takes two minutes:

- Name it after your company or brand
- Invite the people who'll use frndOS with you
- Choose your plan

Once your workspace is live, we'll walk you through adding your first brand.
