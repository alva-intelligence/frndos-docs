**Generic AI output is a choice. Here's how to opt out.**

Every generation in frndOS — every Key Visual, every suggestion — pulls from your **Brand Knowledge**. Without it, AI output is generic and disconnected from your brand strategy. With it, every tool downstream stays consistent, automatically.

Brand Knowledge has three layers:

- **Brand DNA** — the strategic foundation: brand personality, target audience, competitors. This tells the AI *who* your brand is.
- **Brand Identity** — the visual layer: color palette (hex codes welcome), uploaded brand fonts, and visual dos and don'ts.
- **Tonality** — how your brand speaks: tone descriptors, communication dos and don'ts, and sample copy.

Setting it up is straightforward: pick your brand from the top-left selector, open **Brand Knowledge**, and fill in each section. Already have a brand guideline PDF? Upload it and frndOS auto-maps the content to the right fields — review, adjust, done.

You can edit everything later (changes apply to future generations), but the more complete your Brand Knowledge, the more on-brand your output. Worth doing before your first Key Visual.
