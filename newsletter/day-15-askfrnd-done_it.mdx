**Already chatting with AskFrnd? Here's how the pros split the work.**

Think of AskFrnd as the **sketchpad** and the KV Generator as the **studio**. Explore in chat — quick concepts, three visual directions, fast iterations. Then take the winning direction into the **KV Generator** for production: bounding box edits, templates, version history, full Brand Knowledge. Chat for exploration, KV Generator for deliverables. Each refinement in chat is a new generation (and new credits), so be intentional.

**For workspace admins:** credits are yours to steer. Set per-user allocations from the shared pool (heavy KV users get more, viewers get zero), track per-feature and per-user usage in workspace settings, and watch for **credit requests** — members can now self-request more with a note, and approval updates their allocation instantly.
