**Multiple brands in one workspace — you're using frndOS the way agencies do.**

You're already switching brands from the selector, so here's the agency playbook:

**Respect the boundaries you already have.** Brand permissions are enforced where it matters: only users with **Full Access** to a brand can connect its data sources — View-Only users can explore dashboards but not change connections. Keep client teams scoped accordingly.

**Allocate credits like a budget.** Bump allocations for designers on the brand with a big campaign coming; keep client viewers at zero. Track per-user and per-feature usage to refine over time.

**Keep each brand's brain separate.** Independent Brand Knowledge and per-brand KPI targets per brand — never reuse one brand's DNA for another. And always check the active brand before generating; brand context decides where assets land.
