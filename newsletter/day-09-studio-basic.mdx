**From idea to Key Visual, without the blank-canvas dread.**

Time to make something. **Frnd Studio** is your creative production hub, and the **KV Generator** is its flagship tool — taking you from rough concept to production-ready visual, with your Brand Knowledge applied automatically.

Two quick steps before you generate:

**1. Create a Project.** Every KV lives inside a Project — it's what gives the AI campaign-level context. Fill in the Project Brief (audience, visual direction, messaging); the more you put in, the more on-target your generations.

**2. Pick your mode.** The KV Generator has two output types:

- **Sketch** — intentionally rough, built for fast, early-stage ideation
- **Craft** — refined output, closer to production-ready

Start in Sketch while exploring directions; switch to Craft once you've locked a concept. From there, use the **Bounding Box tool** to regenerate just one area of the canvas ("change the background to a plastic bottle") — and don't worry about wrong turns: every generation is saved in version history, so nothing is ever lost.
