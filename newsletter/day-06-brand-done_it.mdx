**Brand created — nicely done. Now let's tune it.**

Your workspace already has Brand Knowledge set up — which means you can skip the basics and go straight to getting more from it. Three ways to go deeper:

**1. Tune your tone of voice.** Beyond tone descriptors, add **sample copy** — real headlines and captions. It's the fastest way to anchor the AI's voice. And when a campaign needs to break from the norm (say, a bold Gen-Z direction), override Tonality at the **project level** instead of touching your global settings.

**2. Let the PDF do the work.** If you haven't yet, upload your brand guideline PDF via **Brand Knowledge → Upload Guideline**. frndOS extracts and maps it automatically — just review the mapped fields.

**3. Check identity completeness.** Colors, *both* display and body fonts, and written visual dos and don'ts ("natural lighting, never harsh shadows"). Even partial identity helps — complete identity compounds.
