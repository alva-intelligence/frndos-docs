**You've seen the pillars. Here's where frndOS scales with you.**

If you handle more than one brand — and if you're at an agency, you do — frndOS was built with that as the default, not the edge case. Switch brands from the **top-left brand selector**: each brand carries its own independent Brand Knowledge, Projects, Insights data, and assets. One click, full context switch, no juggling logins. (Need a new brand added? Your frndOS account representative can set it up.)

And this is a platform that keeps moving. On the roadmap, building on the 2.0 foundation:

- **Auto-generated monthly and quarterly campaign reports**
- **Dynamic KPI configuration** — choose which sources feed which metrics
- **Social listening overlays** in campaign timelines
- **Multi-account connections** per platform per brand

These are in the works — not promises with dates — and the best way to catch them landing is the **What's New** changelog. New features, honest limitations, occasional coffee metaphors.

You've made it through onboarding. Now go make something great.
