**Your data is flowing. Time to organize it like a pro.**

You've already connected a data source — that was the hard part. Now unlock campaign-level reporting:

**Tag your content.** Define custom attributes in Owned Media — we recommend at minimum **Campaign**, **Content Pillar**, and **Objective**. These three map directly to standard report structures. Use **bulk tag** to label a whole month of posts in one move.

**Build campaign views.** The **Campaign tab** uses those tags to aggregate metrics per campaign, so you can compare campaign vs. always-on performance and see results over the campaign timeline.

**Set KPI targets.** In KPI Setup, enter target values per metric (e.g., 1M impressions/month). Targets appear right next to actuals on your dashboards — progress at a glance, per brand.
