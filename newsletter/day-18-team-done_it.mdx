**First teammate in — now let's keep the house in order.**

You've already invited someone. As the workspace grows, three habits pay off:

**The agency pattern: visibility on, approvals strict.** Let legitimate teammates find and request the workspace, but review every request. Don't recognize the email? **Reject** — real members can always be re-invited directly, and rejection never permanently blocks anyone.

**Role hygiene.** Audit **Workspace Settings → Members** periodically: Editors for people producing work, Admin only for those managing settings, Viewer/Guest (with zero credit allocation) for clients and stakeholders. You can change any role at any time.

**Know the boundary.** Approved members can access all brands in the workspace — brand-level granular permissions aren't a self-serve feature yet. For fully separated client access, separate workspaces remain the clean option.
