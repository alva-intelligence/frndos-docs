**A dashboard full of zeros isn't broken — it's waiting.**

If you've opened **Insights** and seen zero values everywhere: that's expected. Insights only comes alive once you connect at least one data source. The good news? It's fully self-serve — no tickets, no waiting.

What you can plug in:

- **Paid Media** — Meta Business and TikTok Ads, connected via OAuth. Log in with your own platform credentials, pick the ad account, done.
- **Owned Media** — Facebook Pages and Instagram Business accounts, same OAuth flow, with your choice of historical window (1 month to 1 year).
- **Earned Media & ATL** — influencer coverage, PR, TV, print, OOH — connected via Google Sheets, with auto-mapped columns.

Once connected, the **Overview tab** unifies everything into a single funnel view — awareness, consideration, conversion — across every channel. Data syncs daily and automatically starting each evening, and you can force a sync when you can't wait.

One honest note: each brand currently supports one connection per platform, and X (Twitter) isn't available yet.
