**Your creative OS. Time to explore every corner.**

Hi there — welcome to frndOS. You've just joined an AI-powered platform built for brands and agencies that want creative content with brand consistency, faster, and at greater scale.

Here's the lay of the land — three pillars, one foundation:

- 🎨 **Studio** — generate Key Visuals, edit, and resize them for every platform, with AI doing the heavy lifting while you stay in creative control.
- 📊 **Insights** — your brand's performance across Paid, Owned, Earned, and ATL media, unified in one dashboard.
- 🔍 **Research** — A/B tests, surveys, and a deep research agent to understand your audience.

Underneath it all sits **Brand Knowledge** — your brand's DNA, identity, and tone of voice, configured once and applied everywhere. And **AskFrnd**, an AI assistant that actually understands your brand context, is one click away on any page.

Your first steps are simple: confirm your account, join or create a workspace, and set up your first brand. By the time you finish your coffee, you'll be generating.
