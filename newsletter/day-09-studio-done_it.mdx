**You've generated your first KV. Now multiply it.**

Nice work — you're already creating in Studio. Here's the next level:

**Resize once, deliver everywhere.** Open your finalized KV, switch to **Resize Mode**, and adapt it to feed, story, and banner sizes in one step. It's not a crop — the composition adapts so important elements stay visible.

**Get surgical in Craft mode.** Attach a **reference image** to control camera angle or composition ("extreme low-angle shot" + reference works far better than words alone). For logos and footers the AI shouldn't touch, use **Add Image** to place static elements on the canvas.

**Stop repeating yourself.** Recurring layout — logo position, footer style? Save it as a **Template** and apply it to every future KV.

And if a new generation comes out worse than the last: version history has your back. Restore any iteration.
