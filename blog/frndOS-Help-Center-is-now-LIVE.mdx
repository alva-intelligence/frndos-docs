The frndOS Help Center is now live at [help.frndos.com](https://help.frndos.com).

## What's new

* **Guides** for every frndOS module — Brand Settings, Brief, Studio, Insights, AskFrnd, and Account
* **Search** across guides and changelog
* **What's New** — a changelog every time the product updates

## How to access it

Open [help.frndos.com](https://help.frndos.com) or click the **Help** link inside the frndOS app.

For anything not covered in the Help Center, ask **AskFrnd** directly inside the app.

Learn how to use this → [Welcome to frndOS](/docs/getting-started/welcome)
