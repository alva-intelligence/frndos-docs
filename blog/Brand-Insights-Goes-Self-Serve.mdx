# Brand Insights Goes Self-Serve

**Your data. Your brand. Your insights — on demand.**

Insights is now fully self-serve. Connect your own brand data sources directly — no more waiting on the data team, no more tickets, no more back-and-forth.

Plug in your accounts, map your brands, and start exploring performance in minutes. Every metric, every trend, every segment — all powered by your own data, refreshed in real time.

This is Insights the way it was meant to be: **fast, flexible, and fully in your hands.**

***

## The Way It Used to Be

Until today, getting your brand data into Insights meant going through us.

You'd raise a ticket. Wait for someone on the data team to read it, prioritize it, queue it. Then exchange spreadsheets, share access tokens, debug failed imports, and finally — maybe a few days later — see your dashboard come alive.

If something changed, you'd start over.

For agencies juggling multiple brands and dozens of campaigns, that workflow was the single biggest bottleneck to actually *using* Insights. The data was there. The dashboards were there. But the gap in the middle slowed everything down.

That gap is now closed.

***

## What's New

From today, you can connect, configure, and explore — entirely on your own.

**Connect Paid Media in one click.** Meta Business and TikTok Ads now use OAuth-based authentication. Log in with your existing platform credentials, pick the ad accounts you want to track, and you're done. No more sharing access tokens. No more access requests in your DMs.

👉 [Connecting Paid Media →](/docs/insights/connecting-paid-media)

**Connect Owned Media just as fast.** Facebook Pages and Instagram Business accounts work the same way. Authorize once, choose your time window (1 month, 3 months, 6 months, 1 year, or custom), and frndOS pulls in your full post history — including comments, replies, and engagement, all the way down.

👉 [Connecting Owned Media →](/docs/insights/connecting-owned-media)

**Bring in Earned Media and ATL via Google Sheets.** For channels without direct API access — influencer coverage, PR, TV ratings, print, programmatic ads — connect your data through Google Sheets with a few clicks. Use our sample sheet templates, or bring your own structure. frndOS auto-maps your columns and you take it from there.

👉 [Connecting Earned Media & ATL →](/docs/insights/connecting-earned-atl)

**See everything together.** Once connected, your Overview tab unifies it all — Paid, Owned, Earned, and ATL — into a single funnel view. Filter by awareness, consideration, conversion. Switch brands with one click. Compare always-on performance to specific campaigns.

***

## What You Can Do Now That You Couldn't Before

The self-serve model changes how you work with data, not just how you connect it.

**Onboard a new brand in minutes, not days.** Got a new client? Add the brand, connect the data sources, and you're producing insights the same afternoon. Demos, pitches, and quarterly reviews don't have to wait for backend setup.

**Tag and organize on your own terms.** Define custom labels — campaign, content pillar, objective, sub-campaign — and tag your posts in bulk or one by one. Use those tags to filter, group, and build campaign-specific reports that match how your team actually thinks.

👉 [Understanding Owned Media & Content Labels →](/docs/insights/owned-media)

**Iterate fast.** Realized you need different KPI targets, a different funnel definition, or a different reporting cut? Change it yourself. The dashboard updates. No ticket, no thread, no delay.

**Stay in real time.** Connected accounts sync daily — automatically, in the background, starting each evening. If you need fresher data, force a sync on demand. Your insights stay as current as your campaigns.

***

## A Quick Walkthrough

Here's the full self-serve flow, end to end:

1. **Pick your brand** from the top-left brand selector — or create a new one
2. **Open Insights** and choose the media type you want to connect (Paid, Owned, Earned, ATL)
3. **Authorize** via OAuth (for Meta, TikTok, Facebook, Instagram) or **paste a Google Sheet URL** (for Earned and ATL)
4. **Select the accounts** or data ranges you want to pull in
5. **Wait for the sync** — initial connections finish in minutes to a few hours depending on data volume
6. **Tag your content** to unlock campaign-level reporting
7. **Explore the dashboards** — Overview, Paid, Owned, Earned, ATL, Campaign

The first five steps usually take under ten minutes. By the time you finish your coffee, you're already inside the data.

## A Few Things Worth Knowing

**Brand permissions are respected.** If you have Full Access to a brand, you can connect data sources for it. View-only users can explore dashboards but not change connections. This keeps the right boundaries in place when teams share workspaces.

**One connection per platform, per brand — for now.** Each brand currently supports one Meta connection and one TikTok Ads connection. If you handle a brand with multiple ad accounts, you can connect one at a time. Multi-account support is in active development.

**X (Twitter) is not yet available.** Direct X integration requires platform-specific approvals that we're still working through. Stay tuned.

**Sync notifications are coming.** For now, check the **last updated** timestamp on each connection to confirm fresh data. We're working on push and chat notifications for sync completion in an upcoming release.
