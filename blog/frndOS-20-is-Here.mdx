**Your creative OS, reimagined. Explore every corner.**

We're thrilled to officially launch frndOS — a smarter, faster, and more connected workspace built for how modern creative teams actually work.

From Studio to Research to Insights, every pillar has been refined to help you move from idea to execution with less friction. New workflows, sharper tools, and a cleaner experience across the board.

Take your time. Click around. Break things (gently). Every update is designed to give you more creative leverage — so enjoy the ride and make something great.

***

## Why 2.0

The first version of frndOS proved the idea: AI-powered tools for creative work, with brand context baked in from the start. It worked. Teams used it. Campaigns shipped faster.

But it was also clear where the seams showed. Tools that felt powerful but disconnected. Workflows that needed too many handoffs. A platform that *almost* let you stay in one place — but not quite.

2.0 closes those gaps. It's the same vision, executed end-to-end.

Three pillars. One brand brain. One workspace. Every tool aware of the others.

***

## 🎨 Studio — Refined for Production

Studio is where ideas become assets. In 2.0, every Studio tool now lives in a unified, brand-aware workspace — and they actually talk to each other.

**KV Generator** is the centerpiece. Generate Key Visuals from concept to craft, with **Sketch mode** for fast ideation and **Craft mode** for production-ready refinement. New **Bounding Box editing** lets you target specific areas of the canvas for targeted changes. Version history is built in — nothing you generate is ever lost.

👉 [Creating Your First Key Visual →](/docs/studio/kv-generator)

**Image Editor** for fine-tuning visuals beyond what the generator produces. **Resizer** adapts your finalized KV to every platform — feed, story, banner, custom — in a single step. No starting over.

👉 [Studio Overview →](/docs/studio/overview)

The whole flow is now brand-aware by default. Every generation inherits your Brand Knowledge. Every variation respects your visual identity. Less prompting, more creating.

***

## 📊 Insights — Now Fully Self-Serve

Insights gets a complete rework. The biggest change: **it's self-serve**.

Connect **Paid Media** (Meta Business, TikTok Ads), **Owned Media** (Facebook, Instagram), **Earned Media** (via Google Sheets), and **ATL** (TV, print, OOH) directly — no tickets, no waiting. OAuth handles authentication for ad platforms. Sample sheet templates make Google-Sheets-based imports painless. Sync runs daily, automatically.

Once connected, your dashboards unify everything into a single funnel view — awareness, consideration, conversion — across every channel. Switch brands with a click. Compare always-on performance to specific campaigns. Tag your content with custom labels for richer reporting.

👉 [Insights Overview →](/docs/insights/overview)

We covered the full self-serve story in its own announcement — check it out for the deep dive.

👉 [Brand Insights Goes Self-Serve →](/blog/brand-insights-self-serve)

***

## 🔍 Research — Built Into the Workflow

Research in 2.0 isn't a separate destination. It's woven into the flow.

Set up **A/B tests** for your creative directions before committing. Run **general surveys** to validate audience assumptions. Define and analyze **audience segments** with structured tooling. Test creative in a **Creative Lab** environment. Or call on the **Deep Research Agent** when you need a question answered without doing the digging yourself.

The point isn't to add more tools. The point is to make audience understanding a habit, not a project — something you reach for as naturally as you reach for the KV Generator.

***

## The Foundation: Brand Knowledge & Projects

The pillars are powerful. What makes them work together is what sits underneath.

**Brand Knowledge** is your brand's brain in frndOS — DNA, Identity, Tonality — configured once and applied everywhere. Upload a PDF brand guideline and frndOS maps it automatically. Adjust at any time. Every tool downstream stays consistent.

👉 [Setting Up Your Brand Profile →](/docs/brand-setup/brand-profile)

**Projects** are how campaigns stay organized. Each project carries its own brief, audience, direction, and tone — and can override brand-level settings when a campaign needs to break from the norm. KVs, reports, and references all live inside the project that birthed them.

👉 [Creating & Managing Projects →](/docs/projects/managing-projects)

Together, they're what makes Studio, Research, and Insights feel like one platform instead of three.

***

## AskFrnd — Your AI Assistant, Everywhere

**AskFrnd** is the conversational layer that ties everything together. Ask questions about your brand, your campaigns, your performance — and get answers grounded in your actual data and Brand Knowledge.

It's already live inside Insights for analyzing posts and campaigns ("what are my top 3 posts this month?", "why did this campaign outperform that one?"). It's growing into the rest of frndOS — show up wherever you'd otherwise need to dig.

This is what AI in a creative OS should feel like: not a chatbot bolted on the side, but a real assistant that knows your work.

***

## Multi-Brand, Built In

If you're at an agency, you're not handling one brand — you're handling many. 2.0 was built with that as the default, not the edge case.

Switch between brands from the **top-left brand selector**. Each brand has its own independent Brand Knowledge, Projects, Insights data, and assets. Permissions are per-brand — Full Access for the teams that own a brand, View-Only for those who just need visibility.

👉 [Managing Multiple Brands →](/docs/brand-setup/multiple-brands)

No more separate workspaces. No more juggling logins. One account, many brands, clear boundaries.

***

## A Cleaner, Faster Experience

Under the hood, 2.0 is faster. On the surface, it's cleaner.

The homepage now centers on what's actually happening — your active brands, your work in progress, what's new. Navigation is simpler. The visual language is calmer. Less chrome, more content.

Every tool you used in v1 is still here, just better positioned and better connected. Nothing's hidden — but nothing's in your way either.

***

## What's Next

This is the launch, not the finish line. The 2.0 foundation unlocks things we've been waiting to build:

* **Auto-generated monthly and quarterly campaign reports** — set up once, delivered automatically
* **Dynamic KPI configuration** — pick which data sources feed which metrics
* **Social listening overlays** in campaign timelines — connect ATL launches to conversation spikes
* **AskFrnd MCP integration** — analyze your own data, in your own tools, with your own questions
* **Multi-account connections per platform per brand** — connect every ad account in one place

We'll be shipping these over the coming weeks and months. Each one builds on the foundation that 2.0 puts in place.
