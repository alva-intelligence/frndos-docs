# Managing Multiple Brands

If you're working at an agency, chances are you handle more than one brand. frndOS is designed for this — each brand has **independent Brand Knowledge, projects, and assets**, all accessible from a single account.

## How Multiple Brands Work

When you log in to frndOS, all brands assigned to your account appear in the **brand selector** at the top-left of the dashboard.

Each brand has its own:
- **Brand Knowledge** (DNA, Identity, Tonality)
- **Projects** and campaigns
- **Generated assets** and history
- **Brand-specific templates**

Switching brands is just one click — no need to log out or change workspaces.

## How to Switch Between Brands

1. Click the **brand selector** in the top-left of the dashboard
2. Choose the brand you want to work in
3. The entire workspace (projects, assets, Brand Knowledge) updates to that brand's context

## Best Practices

### Set Up Brand Knowledge for Each Brand Separately

Don't reuse Brand Knowledge across brands. Each brand should have its own DNA, Identity, and Tonality configured independently — even if some elements (like target audience) overlap.

👉 See: [Setting Up Your Brand Profile](./brand-profile)

### Use Projects to Keep Campaigns Organized

Within each brand, use Projects to separate different campaigns. This keeps briefs, KVs, and references organized by campaign — not just by brand.

👉 See: [Creating & Managing Projects](../projects/managing-projects)

### Be Mindful of Active Brand Context

Always check which brand is selected before generating. Brand context determines:
- Which Brand Knowledge applies
- Where generated assets are saved
- Which projects you can see

## FAQ

**Q: How do I get a new brand added to my account?**
Contact your frndOS account representative. New brands are provisioned by the frndOS team.

**Q: Can I copy Brand Knowledge from one brand to another?**
This is not currently a standard self-serve feature. Reach out to your frndOS team if you have a specific use case.

**Q: Can multiple team members access the same brand?**
Yes. Brand access is managed through workspace settings and permissions.

**Q: What happens if I generate a KV under the wrong brand?**
You can recreate the KV under the correct brand. There's no automatic move feature between brands — Brand Knowledge differences would make a direct transfer unreliable anyway.
