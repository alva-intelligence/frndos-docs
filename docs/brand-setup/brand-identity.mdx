# Defining Your Brand Identity

**Brand Identity** is the visual layer of your Brand Knowledge in frndOS. It defines **how your brand looks** — the colors, typography, and visual direction that make your brand recognizable.

Once configured, frndOS uses these settings to keep every AI-generated visual aligned with your brand's look and feel.

## When to Use This

Set up Brand Identity when:
- You've already completed [Brand DNA](./brand-profile) and are ready for the visual layer
- You have brand guideline materials (color palette, fonts, visual examples) ready to upload
- You're onboarding a new brand into frndOS

## What's Inside Brand Identity

| Field | What to Include | Format |
|---|---|---|
| **Color Palette** | Primary, secondary, and accent colors | Hex codes recommended |
| **Typography** | Fonts used in brand communications | Upload font files directly |
| **Visual Style** | Dos and Don'ts for visual direction | Text + reference images |

## How to Set Up Brand Identity

### Step 1 — Navigate to Brand Identity

1. Log in to **frndOS**
2. Select your brand from the top-left brand selector
3. Go to **Brand Knowledge → Brand Identity**

### Step 2 — Configure Colors

Add your brand's color palette:
- **Primary colors** — main brand colors used most often
- **Secondary colors** — supporting palette
- **Accent colors** — highlights and call-outs

Enter hex codes for precision (e.g., `#FF5733`).

### Step 3 — Upload Brand Fonts

Upload your brand's typography directly to frndOS. The AI will reference these fonts when generating layouts and compositions.

> **Tip:** Upload both display and body fonts if your brand uses different fonts for different purposes.

### Step 4 — Define Visual Dos and Don'ts

Add written guidance on your visual style:
- *"Always use natural lighting"*
- *"Avoid harsh shadows"*
- *"Photography should feel candid, not staged"*

You can also attach reference images to illustrate the desired direction.

## Fast Track: Upload Brand Guideline PDF

Instead of filling in each field manually, you can upload your brand guideline PDF:

1. Go to **Brand Knowledge**
2. Click **Upload Guideline**
3. Select your PDF
4. frndOS automatically extracts and maps the content to Brand Identity fields

Review the auto-mapped fields and adjust as needed.

## How Brand Identity Affects Your Output

Once configured, Brand Identity automatically guides:

- **KV Generator** — color palette and visual style inform every generation
- **Image Editor** — refinements respect brand colors and typography
- **Resizer** — typography stays consistent across platform sizes

## FAQ

**Q: What font formats are supported?**
Standard web font formats are supported (TTF, OTF, WOFF). Check with your frndOS account representative if you have a specific format question.

**Q: Can I update Brand Identity later?**
Yes. Brand Identity can be edited anytime. Changes apply to future generations only.

**Q: What if I don't have a brand guideline yet?**
You can fill in fields manually based on what you know. Even partial Brand Identity is better than none — start with your color palette and main fonts.
