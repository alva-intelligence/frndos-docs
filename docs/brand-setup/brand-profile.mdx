# Setting Up Your Brand Profile (Brand DNA)

**Brand DNA** is the strategic foundation of your brand in frndOS. It tells the AI **who your brand is, who it speaks to, and where it stands** in the market.

This is the first piece of Brand Knowledge you should configure. Without it, AI-generated outputs will be generic and disconnected from your brand strategy.

## When to Use This

Set up Brand DNA when:

* You're onboarding a new brand into frndOS
* Your brand strategy has changed (e.g., new target audience, repositioning)
* You're preparing to generate your first Key Visual

## What's Inside Brand DNA

Brand DNA has three core sections:

| Field                 | What to Include                                 | Example                                           |
| --------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| **Brand Personality** | Adjectives that describe your brand's character | *Bold, trustworthy, youthful*                     |
| **Target Audience**   | Demographics, psychographics, behaviors         | *Urban millennials, 25–35, financially ambitious* |
| **Competitors**       | Key competing brands in your category           | *Brand A, Brand B, Brand C*                       |

## How to Set Up Brand DNA

1. Log in to **frndOS** at [frndos.com](https://frndos.com/)
2. In the **top-left corner**, click the brand selector dropdown
3. Select the brand you want to configure
4. Navigate to **Brand Knowledge → Brand DNA**
5. Fill in each section

### Tip: Skip Manual Entry with PDF Upload

If your brand already has a comprehensive brand guideline PDF, you can upload it directly and let frndOS auto-map the content to Brand DNA fields.

👉 See: **Uploading & Managing Brand Assets** *(coming soon)*

## How Brand DNA Affects Your Output

Once configured, Brand DNA is automatically applied to every AI generation under that brand:

* **KV Generator** uses brand personality to guide visual mood
* **AskFrnd** references target audience when suggesting creative ideas
* **All Studio tools** stay grounded in your brand context

## FAQ

**Q: Can I update Brand DNA later?**
Yes. Brand DNA can be edited anytime. Changes apply to future generations only — previously generated assets are not retroactively updated.

**Q: Do I need to fill in all three fields?**
You can start with whatever you have, but the more complete your Brand DNA, the more accurate the AI output will be. We recommend filling in all three sections before generating your first asset.

**Q: Can I override Brand DNA for a specific campaign?**
Yes. You can override at the project level. See [Creating & Managing Projects](../projects/managing-projects) for how to set campaign-specific direction.
