# Setting Your Brand's Tone of Voice

**Tonality** is the communication layer of your Brand Knowledge. It defines **how your brand speaks** — the personality that comes through in words, captions, and creative direction.

## When to Use This

Set up Tonality when:
- You've configured [Brand DNA](./brand-profile) and [Brand Identity](./brand-identity)
- You want AI-generated direction to match your brand's voice
- You need to override tonality for specific campaigns (see FAQ below)

## What's Inside Tonality

| Field | What to Include | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Tone Descriptors** | Adjectives describing your brand voice | *Friendly, authoritative, playful, formal* |
| **Communication Dos** | What your brand says and how | *"Always speak in second person (you)"* |
| **Communication Don'ts** | What your brand avoids | *"Never use technical jargon"* |
| **Sample Copy** | Examples of on-brand messaging | Actual headlines, captions, or taglines |

## How to Set Up Tonality

1. Log in to **frndOS**
2. Select your brand from the top-left brand selector
3. Go to **Brand Knowledge → Tonality**
4. Fill in each field, or upload your brand guideline PDF to auto-map

### Example: A Bank Brand

```
Tone Descriptors: Trustworthy, approachable, modern
Dos: Use clear, simple language. Speak as a partner, not an institution.
Don'ts: Avoid financial jargon. Don't be overly formal.
Sample Copy: "Your money, your goals, our partnership."
```

## Overriding Tonality for Specific Campaigns

Sometimes a campaign needs to **break from your usual brand voice** — for example, a Gen-Z campaign that's intentionally experimental and bold.

You don't have to change your brand's global Tonality. Instead, override at the project level:

1. Create a **Project** for the campaign
2. In the **Project Brief**, enter a custom tonality
3. Toggle off the default Brand Knowledge for that project
4. Generate as usual — the AI will use your campaign-specific tonality

👉 See: [Creating & Managing Projects](../projects/managing-projects)

## How Tonality Affects Your Output

Once configured, Tonality guides:

- **KV Generator** — AI suggestions and concept direction reflect your voice
- **AskFrnd** — creative ideas and copy suggestions stay on-brand
- **All Studio tools** — written guidance is tone-aware

## FAQ

**Q: Can I have different tonalities for different campaigns?**
Yes. Your brand-level Tonality is the default, but every project can override it. This is especially useful for experimental campaigns that intentionally depart from your usual brand voice.

**Q: Can I update Tonality later?**
Yes. Tonality can be edited anytime. Changes apply to future generations only.

**Q: How is Tonality different from Brand DNA?**
- **Brand DNA** = strategic foundation (personality, audience, competitors)
- **Tonality** = how the brand communicates (voice, words, style)

Both work together. DNA defines *who* you are; Tonality defines *how* you speak.
