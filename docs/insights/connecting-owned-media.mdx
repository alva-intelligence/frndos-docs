# Connecting Owned Media

Owned Media in Insights tracks your brand's **organic social media performance** — posts, comments, reach, and engagement. Currently supported:

- **Facebook Pages**
- **Instagram Business Accounts**

Like Paid Media, the connection uses **OAuth** for secure, credential-free access.

## Before You Start

You need:
- **Admin access** to the social media account (Facebook Page or Instagram Business)
- **Full access** role for the brand in frndOS
- The correct brand selected from the top-left

## How to Connect

### Step 1 — Open Connection Flow

1. Navigate to **Insights → Owned Media**
2. Click **Connect** and choose the platform (Facebook or Instagram)

### Step 2 — Authorize

You'll be redirected to authorize via the platform's OAuth flow. After authorization, you'll see a list of pages or accounts you have access to.

### Step 3 — Choose Account & Time Frame

1. Select the **account** you want to connect (e.g., the Facebook Page or IG Business account)
2. Choose a **time frame** for the historical data pull:
   - 1 month back
   - 3 months back (default)
   - 6 months back
   - 1 year back
   - Custom range

### Step 4 — Wait for Sync

Connection status moves through:
1. **Setting up**
2. **Syncing** — this can take a while because frndOS pulls deep data including **all comments on posts**
3. **Connected**

> **Why does Facebook take longer?** Facebook syncs include comments-on-comments (nested comments), which makes the data pull deeper than Instagram.

## What Data Gets Pulled

Once connected, Owned Media tracks:

| Default Metrics | Description |
|---|---|
| **Views** | Post views/impressions |
| **Comments** | Including nested comments (Facebook) |
| **Likes** | Reactions on posts |
| **Shares** | Re-shares |

You can extend this data with **custom tags and labels** per post — see [Understanding Owned Media & Content Labels](./owned-media).

## Data Sync Behavior

- **Daily automatic sync** runs from **7 PM through early morning**
- Sync status is shown per connection (e.g., "Syncing" badge)
- **Manual force-sync** available if you don't want to wait
- Check **last updated** timestamp to confirm freshness
- Removed connections clear from the dashboard

## Multi-Platform Mixing

When you connect both Facebook and Instagram under one brand, posts from both platforms appear together in the Owned Media tab, with platform indicators per row. This is intentional — you get a unified view of your owned content.

## Reconnecting or Removing

**To remove:**
1. Open the connected account row
2. Click **Remove**

**To reconnect** (e.g., access revoked):
1. Remove the existing connection
2. Run the connection flow again

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: My Page/Account doesn't show in the list.**
- Your Facebook account may not have admin access to the Page
- For Instagram: the account must be a **Business** account linked to a Facebook Page
- Try removing the existing frndOS connection and reconnecting

**Problem: Connection succeeded but data isn't appearing.**
- Initial sync can take time, especially for accounts with many posts
- Check the sync status; if it's still "Syncing", wait
- If it's "Connected" but empty, force a manual sync

**Problem: Mixed data from wrong account.**
If you previously connected a different account (e.g., during testing), data from that account may still be in your view. Remove the old connection completely to clean up.
