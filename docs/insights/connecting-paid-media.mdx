# Connecting Paid Media

Paid Media in Insights tracks performance from your advertising platforms. Currently supported:

- **Meta Business** (Facebook & Instagram Ads)
- **TikTok Ads**

The connection flow uses **OAuth** — you grant frndOS access using your own platform login, no need to share credentials or manage individual user access.

## Before You Start

You need:
- **Login access** to the ad account you want to connect (Meta Business or TikTok Ads Manager)
- **Full access** role for the brand in frndOS (view-only users cannot connect data)
- The correct brand open (click it under **Brands** in the Home sidebar if you haven't already)

## How to Connect Meta Business

### Step 1 — Open the Connection Flow

1. Log in to **frndOS** and select your brand
2. Navigate to **Insights → Paid Media**
3. Click **Connect** and choose **Meta Business**

> **Core or Extended?** When you connect, you'll be asked whether this is a **Core** connection (the brand's own account) or an **Extended** connection (reusing data from another brand). For a brand's primary account, choose **Core**. See [Core vs Extended Connections](./core-extended-connections).

### Step 2 — Authorize Access

You'll be redirected to Facebook to grant frndOS access to your account.

After authorizing, you'll see a list of **ad accounts** you have access to. Select the one you want to connect.

### Step 3 — Wait for Sync

After selection:
1. Status changes to **Setting up**
2. Then **Syncing**
3. Finally **Connected**

Sync time depends on data volume. Initial sync may take a while; subsequent daily syncs are faster.

## How to Connect TikTok Ads

The flow is similar to Meta:

1. From Insights → Paid Media, click **Connect** → **TikTok Ads**
2. Authorize via TikTok Ads Manager
3. Select the ad account
4. Wait for sync to complete

> **Note:** TikTok Ads has known platform-specific issues that occasionally surface. If a connection fails, try again or reach out to support.

## Important Notes & Limitations

### One Core Connection Per Platform Per Brand

Each brand has **one Core connection** per platform for its primary account (one Meta Business Core, one TikTok Ads Core).

If you need to connect **additional accounts** of the same type — or reuse data already connected to another brand in your workspace — use **Extended connections** instead. This avoids duplicate syncing and cost.

👉 See: [Core vs Extended Connections](./core-extended-connections)

### TikTok: Organic + Paid Data Mix

When you connect TikTok at the account level, **organic and paid data are mixed** (TikTok doesn't always cleanly separate them at the API level, unlike Meta).

### X (Twitter) Not Yet Supported

X is not currently supported. It requires a custom developer integration and is on the future roadmap.

## After Connection

Once connected, your Paid Media dashboard will populate with:

- Impressions, reach, engagement, spend (per platform)
- Funnel filtering (Awareness, Consideration, Conversion, EV)
- Time-based filtering (default: last 3 months)
- Per-campaign breakdowns (via the Campaign tab)

## Reconnecting or Removing a Connection

To **remove a connection**:
1. Go to Paid Media settings
2. Find the connected account
3. Click **Remove**

To **reconnect** (e.g., after revoked access):
1. Remove the existing connection
2. Run the connection flow again

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: My ad accounts don't appear after authorizing.**
Possible causes:
- Your platform login doesn't have access to the ad account
- Permissions changed recently on the platform side
- The connection has cached older session data — try removing and reconnecting

**Problem: Connection stuck at "Setting up" for a long time.**
This can happen on initial sync due to data volume. Wait, or contact support if it persists more than a few hours.

**Problem: I can only see "View only" — no Connect button.**
You don't have Full Access for this brand. Contact your workspace admin to upgrade your role.
