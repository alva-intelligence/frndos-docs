# Settings & Administration Overview

**Workspace Settings** is the admin control center for your workspace — where owners and admins manage who's on the team, how credits are spent, billing, and which frndOS capabilities are turned on.

Most of these pages are **admin-only** and apply to the **whole workspace**, so a change here affects every member.

## Where to Find Workspace Settings

1. Log in to **frndOS**
2. Open **Workspace Settings** from the workspace menu
3. Pick the area you need from the settings sidebar — for example, **AskFRND Tools** or **Experimental**

> **Why it's admin-only:** Most settings apply to the entire workspace, so they're gated to owners and admins. If you don't see a page, you likely don't have the permission — or you're in a Personal workspace.

## Admin-Only & Team-Only

Workspace Settings is built for **team workspaces** and **admins**:

- Most pages need the **workspace settings** (or related) permission. Members without it don't see them, and the server enforces the same rule.
- These pages aren't available in a **Personal** workspace.
- Changes are **workspace-wide** — they affect every member, not just you.

## FAQ

**Q: I don't see a settings page (or the whole Settings area).**
Most settings are admin-only and team-only. If you're not an owner/admin, or you're in a Personal workspace, the page won't appear. Ask a workspace admin.

**Q: Do changes here affect my whole team?**
Yes — Workspace Settings applies to the entire workspace, so a change affects every member.

**Q: Where do I manage credits and buy more?**
Under **Credit Usage** (and **Billing** for invoices). See [Managing Workspace Credits](../workspace/managing-credits).
