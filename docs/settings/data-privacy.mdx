# Your Data Rights — Export & Deletion

frndOS lets you **export your personal data** or **request account deletion** from the Privacy Policy page. These actions are personal — they apply to your own account, not the workspace.

> **Heads-up:** Both actions are **request-based**, not instant. A team at frndOS reviews and processes each submission, then contacts you by email.

## Where to Find Data Rights

Data Export and Account Deletion are not inside Workspace Settings — they live on the **Privacy Policy** page:

1. Click your **profile / workspace name** in the sidebar (bottom-left)
2. Under **Misc**, click **Privacy Policy**
3. Scroll to the **Your Rights** section

You'll see two side-by-side cards: **Export My Data** and **Delete My Account**.

## Requesting a Data Export

**Export My Data** sends your personal data to your registered email address within 48 hours.

1. On the **Your Rights** section, click **Request Data Export**
2. A confirmation dialog opens, showing the email address your export will be sent to
3. Review the email address, then click **Submit Request**
4. A success screen confirms: _"Our team will prepare your data and send it to [email] within 48 hours."_
5. Click **Done** to dismiss

> **Heads-up:** If you already have a pending export request, submitting a new one returns an error. Wait for the current request to complete before submitting again.

## Requesting Account Deletion

**Delete My Account** submits a deletion request for review by the frndOS team.

> **Important:** This submits a request — your account is **not deleted immediately**. The team will reach out by email before taking any action. Once deletion is approved and processed, it is **permanent and cannot be undone**.

1. On the **Your Rights** section, click **Request Account Deletion**
2. A dialog opens with a warning banner explaining the process
3. Fill in the required fields:
   - **Reason for leaving** — describe why you're leaving (required)
   - **Type "DELETE" to confirm your request** — type the word `DELETE` exactly (case-insensitive), then the Submit button activates
4. Click **Submit Deletion Request**
5. A success screen confirms: _"Our team will review your account deletion request and notify you via email once it's processed."_
6. Click **Done** to dismiss

> **Heads-up:** If you have a pending deletion request already, submitting again returns an error.

### Cancelling a Deletion Request

If you change your mind, frndOS sends a **cancellation link** to your email. Clicking that link cancels your deletion request and returns you to the Privacy Policy page with a confirmation notice.

## FAQ

**Q: Where do I find data export and account deletion?**
On the **Privacy Policy** page — go to your sidebar, click your profile/workspace name, then **Misc → Privacy Policy**, and scroll to **Your Rights**.

**Q: How long does a data export take?**
Up to 48 hours. You'll receive an email at your registered address when the export is ready.

**Q: Is my account deleted immediately when I submit?**
No. It's a request — the frndOS team reviews it and contacts you by email before taking any action.

**Q: Can I cancel an account deletion request?**
Yes. frndOS emails you a cancellation link after you submit. Clicking it cancels the request.

**Q: I already submitted a deletion request and want to cancel, but I can't find the email.**
Contact [support@frnd.io](mailto:support@frnd.io) for assistance.

**Q: Are these workspace-wide actions?**
No. Export and deletion apply to **your personal account only**, not your workspace or other members.
