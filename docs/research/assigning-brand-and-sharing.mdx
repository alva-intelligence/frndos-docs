# Assigning a Brand & Sharing Surveys

Every survey belongs to a **brand**, and finished surveys can be **shared** with everyone in your workspace. Both are managed from the survey itself.

> Looking for the basics first? See [Creating a General Survey](./creating-a-general-survey) and [Managing & Tracking Your Surveys](./managing-surveys).

## Assigning a Brand

A survey's brand is shown as **Client** on the Surveys list and as **Brand** on the survey's **Detail** tab. It's normally set to whichever brand was active when you created the survey.

If a survey has **no brand yet**, the Brand field is marked **Required** — assign one so the survey is filed under the right brand.

**To set or change the brand:**

1. Open the survey and go to the **Detail** tab
2. Click the **Brand** field (it shows the current brand, or _"Choose Brand"_)
3. Pick a brand from the list — the change saves immediately

### Creating a Brand on the Spot

Don't have the right brand yet? You can create one without leaving the survey:

1. Open the **Brand** dropdown
2. Type a new brand name in the **Search / Add Brand** field
3. If no brand matches, an **add** option appears — press **Enter** to create it

The new brand is created and assigned to the survey in one step.

> **Tip:** Search first before adding — the field filters existing brands as you type, so you don't create a duplicate.

## Sharing a Survey with Your Workspace

By default, a survey is only visible to you within its brand. You can make a survey **public** so everyone in the workspace can see it — it then appears under their **Shared to you** tab.

**To share a survey:**

1. On the Surveys list, open the survey's action menu
2. Turn on **Share this file to all members** — _"Allow everyone to see this file"_

Turn the toggle off again to make it private.

> **Heads-up:** Sharing is only available once a survey has results worth sharing — you can share **Launched**, **Completed**, and **Terminated** surveys. **Draft** and **Processing** surveys can't be shared yet.

## FAQ

**Q: The Brand field says "Required" — what does that mean?**
The survey isn't attached to any brand. Assign one from the Brand dropdown on the Detail tab so it's filed correctly.

**Q: Can I move a survey to a different brand?**
Yes. Open the survey's Detail tab, click the Brand field, and pick another brand. The change is immediate.

**Q: I created a brand by accident from the survey. How do I manage it?**
Brands you create here are regular brands — manage them like any other. See [Working with Multiple Brands](../brand-setup/multiple-brands).

**Q: Why can't I share my Draft survey?**
Sharing only applies to Launched, Completed, and Terminated surveys. Launch it first, then share.

**Q: Where do shared surveys show up for other people?**
Under the **Shared to you** tab on their Surveys list.
