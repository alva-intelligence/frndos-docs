# Exploring Customers & Segments

Once your customers are imported and enriched, two tabs let you explore them: **Customer Table View** (every customer, searchable and filterable) and **Segment List** (your audience grouped into segments).

> Haven't imported yet? See [Importing & Enriching Customers](./importing-customers).

## Customer Table View

The **Customer Table View** lists every enriched customer for the active brand. Each row shows:

| Column | What it shows |
| ------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| **Customer Name** | The customer's name |
| **Description** | A short AI description of the customer |
| **AI Segment** | AI-assigned segment tags |
| **Status** | Active, Risk, or Churned |
| **Processing Status** | Whether the customer is enriched yet |
| **Passion Points** | The customer's interests |
| **Brand Product** | Your products the customer engages with |
| **Other Product** | Other (competitor / adjacent) products |
| **Intent** | Purchase-intent score |
| **CLV** | Customer lifetime value |

### Search and Filter

- **Search** — use the search box (_"Search customers…"_) to find a customer by name.
- **Filters** — narrow the list by:
  - **Status** — Active, Risk, Churned
  - **Processing Status** — Unprocessed, Processing, Processed, Error

When no customers exist yet, the tab shows **"No Customer Data Available"** — import a CSV first.

### Opening a Customer Profile

Open any customer to see their full profile, including enriched detail such as:

- **Social profiles** — platform handles, followers, posts, content themes
- **Sentiment** — brand affinity, brand sentiment (per platform), competitor sentiment
- **Scores** — confidence, quality, and accuracy of the enrichment

## Segment List

The **Segment List** groups your customers into segments. Each segment card is one of two types:

- **AI Generated Segments** — segments frndOS builds automatically from your data
- **Manual Segment** — segments you define yourself

The top of the tab summarizes your segments with **Total Segments** and **Top Segments**. Open any segment (**View Segment**) to see the customers inside it.

## FAQ

**Q: What does Processing Status mean?**
It tells you whether a customer has been enriched yet — Unprocessed, Processing, Processed, or Error. Only enriched customers have full profile detail.

**Q: What's the difference between AI Segment and Segment List?**
The **AI Segment** column tags an individual customer. The **Segment List** tab groups customers into named segments (AI-generated or manual).

**Q: A customer shows no interests or scores.**
They likely haven't been enriched yet (check Processing Status), or enrichment failed for that record. See [Importing & Enriching Customers](./importing-customers).
