# Managing & Tracking Your Surveys

Once you've started a survey, everything you do with it afterwards — finding it, watching its progress, reading results — happens on the **Surveys** page inside Research.

> New here? Start with [Creating a General Survey](./creating-a-general-survey).

## The Surveys List

Go to **Research → Surveys**. You'll land on a list titled _"Create and manage your surveys to gather audience insights."_

<!-- TODO: add screenshot of the Surveys list → /img/guides-asset/<name>.jpeg -->

**Two tabs organize your surveys:**

- **Recent Files** — surveys created in this brand
- **Shared to you** — surveys other people made public to you (see [Assigning a Brand & Sharing Surveys](./assigning-brand-and-sharing))

Use the **search bar** to find a survey by name. Each row shows:

| Column | What it shows |
| ------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| **Survey Name** | The survey's title |
| **Client** | The brand the survey belongs to |
| **Status** | Where the survey is in its lifecycle |
| **Respondents** | How many people have responded |
| **Created By** | Who started the survey |
| **Created** | When it was created |
| **Last Updated** | When it last changed |

## Survey Status

Every survey carries a **status** so you always know where it stands:

| Status | Meaning |
| ------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| **Draft** | Created but not launched yet |
| **Processing** | Being set up / prepared — launches when ready |
| **Launched** | Live and collecting responses |
| **Completed** | Finished, results ready |
| **Terminated** | Stopped early |

> **Heads-up:** A survey in **Processing** is still being prepared and will launch automatically once it's ready — you don't need to do anything.

## Opening a Survey

Click any survey in the list to open it. Inside, you'll see up to two tabs.

### Overview

The **Overview** tab gives you the survey at a glance, in four cards:

| Card | What it means |
| --------------- | ------------------------------------------ |
| **Responden** | Respondents who have completed the survey |
| **Pertanyaan** | Number of questions in the survey |
| **Durasi Survey** | How long the survey runs |
| **Total Credit** | Credits spent on this survey |

### Detail

The **Detail** tab lists the survey's full information:

- **Brand** — the brand this survey belongs to (you can set or change this — see [Assigning a Brand & Sharing Surveys](./assigning-brand-and-sharing))
- **Nama Survey** — the survey title
- **Deskripsi** — the survey description
- **Jenis Responden** — the respondent type
- **Status Survey** — the current status
- **Waktu Diluncurkan** — when it was launched
- **Waktu Selesai** — when it finished
- **Creator** — who created it

> **Note:** The **Detail** tab only appears once a survey is past **Processing**. A survey still processing shows the **Overview** only.

## Deleting a Survey

You can delete a survey from its row's action menu on the Surveys list — but **only while it's a Draft or Processing**. Once a survey is **Launched**, **Completed**, or **Terminated**, it can't be deleted; you can still open it to view results.

## FAQ

**Q: A survey is stuck on Processing. What do I do?**
Nothing — Processing means it's still being prepared and will launch on its own once ready. Check back shortly.

**Q: Why can't I delete my survey?**
Only Draft and Processing surveys can be deleted. Launched, Completed, and Terminated surveys are kept so their results stay available.

**Q: Where do I read the actual responses?**
Open the survey and use its Detail view. The response breakdown is shown in the embedded survey tool. _[TODO: confirm what the response/results view shows once available — screenshot needed.]_

**Q: A colleague's survey isn't in my list.**
