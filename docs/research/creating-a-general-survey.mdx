# Creating a General Survey

**Surveys** let you gather real audience insights before you launch a campaign — or after, to measure how it landed. You create surveys from the Research module, and frndOS keeps every survey tied to the brand you're working on.

> This guide covers **starting** a survey. To follow, track, and read results afterwards, see [Managing & Tracking Your Surveys](./managing-surveys). To attach a brand or share a survey, see [Assigning a Brand & Sharing Surveys](./assigning-brand-and-sharing).

## Before You Start

Make sure these are in place first:

1. ✅ **Open the right brand** — from Home, click it under **Brands** in the sidebar. Every survey you create is tied to the active brand.
2. ✅ **You're in a team workspace** — surveys aren't available in a Personal workspace. If you're in one, frndOS will send you back to Home.
3. ✅ **Survey access enabled** — your role needs survey permission. If you don't see **General Survey** in Research, or the **New Survey** button on the Surveys list, ask a workspace admin. See [Inviting Team Members](../workspace/inviting-team-members).
4. ✅ **Enough credits** — surveys are paid from your credit balance. The **New Survey** button is disabled when your balance is too low. See [Managing Workspace Credits](../workspace/managing-credits).

## Three Ways to Start a Survey

frndOS gives you three entry points — they all land in the same survey builder.

**1. From the Research hub**
Open **Research** and click the **General Survey** tool card.

**2. From the Surveys list**
Go to **Research → Surveys** and click **New Survey** (top-right).

**3. From an AskFrnd suggestion**
When AskFrnd suggests running a survey, click its suggestion. frndOS opens the survey builder and can **pre-fill** parts of it based on what you were discussing — so you start with a head start instead of a blank form.

## Cost and Launch

Before it goes live, the survey's cost is charged in **credits**, based on the number of questions and target respondents.

Once launched, the survey moves out of **Draft** and starts collecting responses. From there, follow it in [Managing & Tracking Your Surveys](./managing-surveys).

> **Heads-up:** After a survey is launched you can no longer delete it — you can still open it to track results.

## FAQ

**Q: I don't see the New Survey button or the General Survey card. Why?**
Either your role doesn't have survey access, or you're in a Personal workspace (surveys are team-only). Check that you're in a team workspace and ask an admin about permissions.

**Q: The New Survey button is greyed out.**
Your credit balance is too low to start a survey. Top up credits, then try again. See [Managing Workspace Credits](../workspace/managing-credits).

**Q: AskFrnd suggested a survey — what happens if I click it?**
It opens the survey builder and may pre-fill some fields from your conversation. The pre-fill is a starting point only; edit anything before you launch.

**Q: Which brand does my survey belong to?**
The brand that was active when you created it. You can change it later — see [Assigning a Brand & Sharing Surveys](./assigning-brand-and-sharing).
