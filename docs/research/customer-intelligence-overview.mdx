# Customer Intelligence Overview

**Customer Intelligence** turns a raw customer list into an enriched, AI-understood view of your audience. You bring your customers in (via CSV), frndOS enriches each one — social profiles, interests, sentiment, lifetime value, purchase intent — and you explore them as individuals, as segments, and as insights.

Everything is **scoped per brand**: the brand active in the sidebar decides which customers and segments you see, and gives the AI the brand context it needs for sentiment analysis.

> **Availability:** Customer Intelligence is rolled out gradually behind a feature flag. If you don't see it for your brand, it isn't enabled for your workspace yet.

## The At-a-Glance Overview

When your brand has data, the top of the page shows four summary cards:

| Card | What it shows |
| ------------------------ | ------------------------------------- |
| **Total Customer** | How many customers you've imported |
| **Active Segments** | How many segments are active |
| **Avg. Lifetime Value** | Average customer lifetime value (CLV) |
| **Avg. Purchase Intent** | Average purchase-intent score |

The overview can also surface **suggested actions** — click one to open AskFrnd with a prompt already filled in, so you can dig deeper in chat.

Use **Run History** (top-right) to see past enrichment runs.

## How It's Organized

Customer Intelligence has four tabs:

| Tab | What it's for |
| ------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Integrations** | Bring customers in — upload a CSV and enrich their profiles |
| **Customer Table View** | Browse, search, and filter every enriched customer |
| **Segment List** | See AI-generated and manual segments of your audience |
| **Customer Insights** | Ask questions of your customer base and get AI insights |

New brands with no data yet start on **Integrations** — that's where you begin.

## Before You Start

1. ✅ **Open the right brand** — from Home, click it under **Brands** in the sidebar. Customers, segments, and insights are all scoped to the active brand.
2. ✅ **Customer Intelligence is enabled** for your workspace (see availability note above).
3. ✅ **Enough credits** — enriching customer profiles is paid from your credit balance. See [Managing Workspace Credits](../workspace/managing-credits).

## The Workflow

```
1. Import customers (Integrations → Upload CSV)
        ↓
2. Enrich profiles (AI fills in social, interests, sentiment, CLV, intent)
        ↓
3. Explore (Customer Table View + per-customer profiles)
        ↓
4. Segment (Segment List — AI-generated + manual)
        ↓
5. Get insights (Customer Insights)
```

## FAQ

**Q: I don't see Customer Intelligence for my brand.**
It's rolled out gradually behind a feature flag. If it isn't visible, it hasn't been enabled for your workspace yet.

**Q: Does it cost credits?**
Yes — enriching customer profiles is billable, charged from your workspace credit balance. See [Importing & Enriching Customers](./importing-customers).

**Q: Is my data separated per brand?**
Yes. Everything is scoped to the brand active in the sidebar. Switch brands to see a different customer base.
