# Getting Customer Insights

The **Customer Insights** tab turns your enriched customer base into answers. You describe an audience with a few dropdowns, and frndOS shows you a breakdown of the people who match.

> Insights work on enriched customers. If you haven't imported yet, start with [Importing & Enriching Customers](./importing-customers).

## Step 1 — Describe Your Audience

At the top of the tab, build a question in plain language by filling in three dropdowns:

- **Who** — the group or segment you're interested in
- **Aged** — an age range
- **Passion Points** — an interest to focus on

The options come from your own enriched customer data.

## Step 2 — Show the Results

Click **Show Filter Results**. frndOS analyzes the matching customers and returns a breakdown, including:

- **Summary cards** — headline numbers for the selected audience
- **Gender Distribution**
- **Passion Points**
- **Platform Relevances** — which platforms this audience is active on
- **Brand Products** and **Other Products** they engage with

## FAQ

**Q: The dropdowns are empty.**
The options are built from your enriched customers. If you have few or no enriched customers, there's little to filter on — import and enrich more first. See [Importing & Enriching Customers](./importing-customers).

**Q: How is this different from the Customer Table View?**
The **Table View** lists individual customers. **Customer Insights** aggregates a filtered audience into a breakdown (distributions, top platforms, products).

**Q: Can I explore this further in chat?**
Yes — the Customer Intelligence overview can hand a suggested question to AskFrnd. See [Customer Intelligence Overview](./customer-intelligence-overview).
