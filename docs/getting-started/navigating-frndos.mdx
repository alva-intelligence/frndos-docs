# Navigating frndOS — The Sidebar

frndOS navigation lives in a **left sidebar**. This is where you move between your main areas, switch brands, and open AskFrnd. This guide covers what's in the sidebar and how to get around.

> **Coming from the old layout?** Navigation used to sit in a top bar with thumbnail icons. It's now consolidated into the left sidebar, and the brand switcher has moved there too. Everything you used before is still here — just in a cleaner, more familiar place.

## What's in the Sidebar

The left sidebar gives you quick access to your main navigation areas:

| Item | What It's For |
|---|---|
| **Home** | Your overview — all activity and files across the brands you have access to |
| **Search** | Find brands, files, projects, and pages across your workspace |
| **Chat (AskFrnd)** | Open your AI assistant — see the two modes below |
| **Brand Switcher** | Switch between the brands you work on |
| **Pillars** | Access Studio, Insights, Research, and other pillars |

> **Note:** The sidebar is actively evolving as frndOS grows. Some entries may appear or change as new capabilities roll out. This guide focuses on the stable, everyday navigation you'll use most.

## Home

**Home** is your starting point. It shows all the activity and files related to the brands you have access to — so even if you've just joined a workspace, you can see what's happening across your brands at a glance.

From Home you can search and navigate to other areas of frndOS.

## Switching Brands

The **brand switcher** lives in the sidebar. It shows the brands you have access to and lets you switch the active brand with a click.

Switching brands changes your whole context:
- Which projects and files you see
- Which Brand Knowledge applies
- Which Insights data loads
- Which credit context is active

> **Heads-up:** Creating a new brand is intentionally being given a more guided flow. Don't expect to spin up a brand in a single click — brand setup is a deliberate process so your Brand Knowledge is configured properly. See [Setting Up Your Brand Profile](../brand-setup/brand-profile).

## Two Ways to Use Chat (AskFrnd)

One of the most useful things to understand about the new navigation: **AskFrnd has two modes**, and which one you get depends on how you open it.

### Mode 1 — Side Panel

Open AskFrnd from the **right side** of any page and it appears as a **panel** alongside your current view. This is the context-aware mode — it knows which pillar and data you're looking at, so it's ideal for asking questions about what's on screen.

### Mode 2 — Full Screen

Open Chat from the **left sidebar** and you get a **full-screen chat view** — a dedicated space for longer conversations, with your chat history accessible.

> **Which should I use?** Use the **side panel** when you want to ask about what you're currently viewing (e.g., analyzing an Insights dashboard). Use the **full-screen view** when you want a focused, standalone chat session.

Both modes share your conversation history. See [AskFrnd Overview](../askfrnd/overview) for more.

## Pillars

The pillars — **Studio**, **Insights**, **Research**, and others — are accessible from the sidebar. Each pillar is a workspace for a different kind of work:

- **Studio** → creative production (KV Generator, Resizer)
- **Insights** → performance tracking and dashboards
- **Research** → audience understanding and customer intelligence

👉 See: [Welcome to frndOS](./welcome) for an overview of the pillars.

## FAQ

**Q: I still see the old navigation layout. Why?**
The new sidebar navigation is now in production. If you see an older layout, try refreshing or clearing your cache. If it persists, report it through your agency support group.

**Q: Where did the brand switcher go?**
It moved into the sidebar. Look for your brand list there instead of the old top-left dropdown.

**Q: What's the difference between Chat in the sidebar and the AskFrnd panel?**
Same assistant, two modes. Sidebar Chat opens full-screen; the right-side panel opens alongside your current view and is context-aware. History is shared between them.

**Q: Some sidebar items look empty or unfinished.**
A few areas are still being built out. This guide focuses on the stable navigation. New capabilities will be documented as they ship.
