# Navigating frndOS — The Sidebar

frndOS navigation lives in a **left sidebar**. This is where you move between your main areas, switch brands, and open AskFrnd. This guide covers what's in the sidebar and how to get around.

> **Coming from the old layout?** Navigation used to sit in a top bar with thumbnail icons. It's now consolidated into the left sidebar — brands now live in a **Brands** list there too. Everything you used before is still here — just in a cleaner, more familiar place.

## Two Sidebar States: Home vs. Inside a Brand

This is the most important thing to understand about frndOS navigation: **the sidebar changes shape depending on whether you're at Home or inside a specific brand.** Pillars (Studio, Insights, Research, etc.) only appear once you've entered a brand — they're not available at the Home level.

### Home Sidebar

When you log in, you land on **Home** with this sidebar:

| Item | What It's For |
|---|---|
| **Home** | Your overview — all activity and files across the brands you have access to |
| **Search** | Find brands, files, projects, and pages across your workspace |
| **Agents** | AI agents (beta) |
| **Inbox / Playground / Pitch** | Upcoming areas, still being built |
| **Brands** | A list of every brand you have access to — click one to enter it |
| **Chat** | Your recent AskFrnd conversations not tied to any brand, plus **New Chat** |

> **Note:** There are no pillars (Studio, Insights, etc.) visible at this level. To reach them, click into a brand from the **Brands** list.

### Brand Sidebar

Once you click a brand under **Brands**, the entire sidebar transforms to that brand's context:

| Item | What It's For |
|---|---|
| **Brand name + settings gear** | Replaces the frndOS logo at the top — confirms which brand is active |
| **Back to Home** | Returns you to the Home sidebar |
| **Pillars** (Insights, Studio, Research, Growth, Loyalty) | Each pillar expands to show its tools — e.g., Insights expands into Overview, Campaigns, Ads, Social Media, Influencer, ATL, Trend Signals, Social Listening |
| **Chat** | Recent AskFrnd conversations scoped to **this brand only**, plus **New Chat** |

> **Heads-up:** Pillars are **brand-gated** — you must have a brand open to access Studio, Insights, Research, Growth, or Loyalty. There's no "pillar view" that works across all brands at once; each brand has its own Studio, its own Insights, and so on.

> **Note:** The sidebar is actively evolving as frndOS grows. Some entries may appear or change as new capabilities roll out. This guide focuses on the stable, everyday navigation you'll use most.

## Home

**Home** is your starting point. It shows all the activity and files related to the brands you have access to — so even if you've just joined a workspace, you can see what's happening across your brands at a glance.

From Home you can search and navigate to other areas of frndOS.

## Entering and Switching Brands

To work on a brand, click it under **Brands** in the Home sidebar. This takes you into that brand's dedicated sidebar (Pillars, Chat scoped to that brand, etc.) — see [Brand Sidebar](#brand-sidebar) above.

Entering a brand changes your whole context:
- Which projects and files you see
- Which Brand Knowledge applies
- Which Insights data loads
- Which credit context is active

**To switch to a different brand**, click **Back to Home** at the top of the Brand Sidebar, then pick another brand from the **Brands** list.

> **Heads-up:** Creating a new brand is intentionally being given a more guided flow. Don't expect to spin up a brand in a single click — brand setup is a deliberate process so your Brand Knowledge is configured properly. See [Setting Up Your Brand Profile](../brand-setup/brand-profile).

## Two Ways to Use Chat (AskFrnd)

One of the most useful things to understand about the new navigation: **AskFrnd has two access points, and they show different chat history.**

### Mode 1 — Side Panel (FAB)

Open AskFrnd from the **floating button (FAB) in the bottom-right corner** of any page and it appears as a panel alongside your current view. This mode is context-aware — it knows which pillar and data you're looking at. Its history view shows **every chat you've had, across every brand and pillar** — useful when you can't remember which brand a past conversation happened in.

### Mode 2 — Full Screen (Sidebar)

Open **Chat** from the **left sidebar** for a dedicated, full-screen conversation view. Its history is **scoped to where you currently are**: from Home, you'll see chats not tied to any brand; from inside a brand, you'll see only that brand's chats.

> **Which should I use?** Use the **FAB** when you want to ask about what you're currently viewing, or when you need to find a chat regardless of which brand it was in. Use the **sidebar Chat** when you want a focused session scoped to your current brand (or Home).

👉 See: [AskFrnd Overview](../askfrnd/overview) for the full breakdown of history scope per access point.

## Pillars

The pillars — **Insights**, **Studio**, **Research**, **Growth**, and **Loyalty** — are each a workspace for a different kind of work. **You can only access them from inside a brand** (see [Brand Sidebar](#brand-sidebar) above):

- **Insights** → performance tracking and dashboards (expands into Overview, Campaigns, Ads, Social Media, Influencer, ATL, Trend Signals, Social Listening)
- **Studio** → creative production (KV Generator, Resizer)
- **Research** → audience understanding and customer intelligence
- **Growth** → growth and campaign tools
- **Loyalty** → loyalty program tools

Every pillar is scoped to whichever brand is currently active. There's no cross-brand pillar view — switch brands to switch which brand's Studio, Insights, etc. you're looking at.

👉 See: [Welcome to frndOS](./welcome) for an overview of the pillars.

## FAQ

**Q: I still see the old navigation layout. Why?**
The new sidebar navigation is now in production. If you see an older layout, try refreshing or clearing your cache. If it persists, report it through your agency support group.

**Q: Where did the brand switcher go?**
There isn't a quick dropdown switcher — brands live in the **Brands** list on the Home sidebar. Click a brand to enter it; click **Back to Home** to pick a different one.

**Q: I can't find Studio or Insights in the sidebar. Where are they?**
Pillars only appear once you're inside a brand. From Home, click a brand under **Brands**, and the sidebar will switch to show Insights, Studio, Research, Growth, and Loyalty for that brand.

**Q: What's the difference between Chat in the sidebar and the AskFrnd panel?**
Same assistant, different history scope. The **FAB** (bottom-right) shows history across every brand and pillar. **Sidebar Chat** shows history scoped to where you are — Home-only, or that specific brand only.

**Q: Some sidebar items look empty or unfinished.**
A few areas are still being built out. This guide focuses on the stable navigation. New capabilities will be documented as they ship.
