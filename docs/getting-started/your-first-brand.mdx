# Setting Up Your First Brand

To create your first brand:

1. Click **+ New Brand** on the dashboard
2. Fill in the brand name and industry
3. Upload your logo and key assets
4. Define a short tone of voice
5. Save and continue to [Brand Setup](../brand-setup/brand-profile) for the full setup
