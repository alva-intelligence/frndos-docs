# Welcome to frndOS

frndOS is an AI-powered platform for brands and agencies. It helps you produce creative content with brand consistency, faster, and at greater scale.

## What you can do

- 🏢 **Set up your brand profile** — Brand DNA, identity, tone of voice, assets, talents
- 🎨 **Generate creative with Studio** — KV Generator, Image Editor, Storyboard, Resizer, Campaign Creator
- 📊 **See brand performance** — Insights, trends, campaign reports
- 🔍 **Research your audience** — A/B testing, surveys, deep research agent
- 💬 **Ask AskFrnd** — an AI assistant that understands your brand context
- 📢 **Distribute creative** — social ads and WhatsApp blast
