# AskFrnd Overview

**AskFrnd** is your AI assistant in frndOS. It's not a separate tool — it's a persistent panel you can open anywhere in the platform to ask questions, generate visuals, or get analysis.

If you used the old standalone chat page in v1, AskFrnd is what replaces it — and it's smarter, more accessible, and aware of where you are.

## Where to Find AskFrnd — and What History You'll See

There are two ways to open AskFrnd, and **the chat history you see depends on where you access it from**:

### 1. From the Sidebar — Scoped to Your Current Context

Click **Chat** in the left sidebar. What you see depends on where you are:

- **From Home** → clicking **See all** shows chat history that **isn't tied to any specific brand** — chats created while you were in Home context. These are tagged **Home**.
- **From inside a Brand** → clicking **See all** shows chat history **tied to that specific brand only**. Each entry is tagged with the brand name (and the module it came from, e.g. "Insights").

In other words, sidebar history is **scoped to wherever you currently are** — Home or a specific brand.

### 2. From the Floating AskFrnd Button (FAB) — Everything, Everywhere

The sparkle icon in the **bottom-right corner** is available from any page. Open it and check its history — it shows **every chat you've had, across every brand and every pillar**, regardless of where you currently are.

> **Why does the FAB show everything?** If you manage several brands, you might not remember which brand a past conversation happened in. Rather than opening each brand one by one to search, the FAB's history gives you one place to find any chat across your whole account.

### Quick Reference

| Access Point | History Scope | Tagged By |
|---|---|---|
| Sidebar Chat → Home | Chats not tied to a brand | "Home" |
| Sidebar Chat → inside a Brand | Chats tied to that brand only | Brand name + module |
| FAB (bottom-right, anywhere) | All chats, every brand, every pillar | Brand name or "Home" + module |

> **Filter tip:** All history views let you filter by **Your chat** and **Your module**. The FAB's global view adds a third filter, **Your Brand**, since it spans multiple brands — the sidebar-scoped views don't need it.

## What Makes AskFrnd Different

### It Knows Where You Are

AskFrnd is **context-aware**. When you open the panel from inside the Insights dashboard, it knows you're looking at Insights data. When you open it from Studio, it understands you're in a creative context.

This means:

* **From Insights** → AskFrnd can analyze your dashboard data ("what are my top 3 posts?", "why did this campaign outperform that one?")
* **From Studio** → AskFrnd can help with creative ideation, image generation, or video generation
* **From Home** → AskFrnd works as a general-purpose assistant across your brand and projects

> **In short:** ask the same question from different places, get more relevant answers.

### Conversation History

AskFrnd **saves your chat history** — but what you see depends on your access point. See [Where to Find AskFrnd](#where-to-find-askfrnd--and-what-history-youll-see) above for the full breakdown: Home-scoped, brand-scoped, or everything via the FAB.

### Model Choice

You can choose which **AI model** powers your conversation. Different models have different strengths — some are faster, some are better for visual generation, some are better for analysis. AskFrnd shows the available models in the panel; pick the one that fits your task.

> **Tip:** Credit cost can vary by model. See [Managing Workspace Credits](../workspace/managing-credits) for how credits work.

## AskFrnd vs. KV Generator

A common question: when should you use AskFrnd's image generation vs. opening the KV Generator?

| Use AskFrnd Chat When…                      | Use KV Generator When…                            |
| ------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------- |
| You want a quick concept or exploration     | You're building a finalized Key Visual            |
| You don't need fine canvas controls         | You need bounding box edits, layers, or templates |
| You're chatting and want to generate inline | You need version history and craft refinement     |
| You want flexibility in prompting           | You want repeatable, brand-aware production       |

Think of AskFrnd as the **sketchpad** and KV Generator as the **studio**. Both are valid; they're built for different moments.

## What AskFrnd Knows About Your Brand

AskFrnd has access to:

* Your **active brand** (whichever brand you currently have open — see [Navigating frndOS](../getting-started/navigating-frndos) for how brand context works)
* The **current page context** (which pillar, which data, which view)
* Your **conversation history**, scoped to your access point (see above)

> **Heads-up:** Brand Knowledge (DNA, Identity, Tonality) is currently **only fully consumed by the KV Generator**, not yet by AskFrnd's chat-based generation. This is being worked on. For now, if you need brand-perfect output, use the KV Generator or include brand specifics in your prompt.

## Privacy & History

- Your AskFrnd conversations are stored in your account
- Workspace admins can see usage analytics (how many credits, which features), but **not the content** of your conversations
- Where you can **see** a given conversation depends on your access point — see [Where to Find AskFrnd](#where-to-find-askfrnd--and-what-history-youll-see) above
- Use the **FAB's global history** (bottom-right) when you're not sure which brand a past conversation happened in

## FAQ

**Q: Is AskFrnd the same as the old chat in frndOS v1?**
It evolved from that, yes — but it's redesigned. The biggest changes: it's now a persistent panel (not a separate page), and it's context-aware based on which pillar you're using.

**Q: How much does AskFrnd cost in credits?**
Credit cost depends on the model and the type of action. Image and video generation cost more than text-only conversation. Cost is shown **after** generation completes.

**Q: Can AskFrnd see my campaign data?**
Yes, when you open AskFrnd from inside the Insights pillar — it can analyze the campaign and post data visible in your dashboards.

**Q: Can I switch models mid-conversation?**
Check the model selector in the AskFrnd panel. Behavior may vary based on the specific implementation.

**Q: My AskFrnd panel is gone — where did it go?**
The FAB (floating button) is always available in the bottom-right corner — click it to reopen the panel. You can also open **Chat** from the left sidebar for the full-screen view.

**Q: Why does my chat history look different depending on where I open it?**
History scope depends on your access point. Sidebar Chat shows history scoped to where you are (Home-only or that specific brand). The FAB (bottom-right) always shows everything — every brand, every pillar. See [Where to Find AskFrnd](#where-to-find-askfrnd--and-what-history-youll-see) above.

**Q: I can't find a chat I had last week — where should I look?**
If you're not sure which brand it happened in, use the **FAB's history** (bottom-right corner) — it searches across all your brands and pillars at once, so you don't have to check each brand individually.

**Q: Can AskFrnd use custom brand fonts in generated visuals?**
Not yet — Brand Knowledge fonts are currently only used by the KV Generator. For chat-based generation, you can upload custom fonts directly into your prompt or context.
